Step 3.2: Remove stop words

"Stopwords" are words which have very little meaning. In NLP it's important to remove anything text that only apply noise to the model. Stop words are typically words that are prepositions and conjunctions. Icluding regular expressions removes additional meaningless items like punctuation marks, numbers, etc.

Step 3.3: Tokenize, Vectorize and TF-IDF

Tokenize: Split text into words. Vectorize and TF-IDF: Convert our overviews into a number (vector).
For this purpose we are using scikit-learn tool with TfidfVectorizer library which allows to make those activities into one step.

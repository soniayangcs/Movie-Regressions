Step 3.2: Remove stop words

"Stopwords" are words which have very little meaning. In NLP it's important to remove anything that doesn't have enough sense in a text, such as: prepositions and conjunctions. We can include regular expressions to remove: punctuation marks, numbers, etc.

Step 3.3: Tokenize, Vectorize and TF-IDF

Tokenize: Split text into words. Vectorize and TF-IDF: Convert our overviews into a number (vector).
For this purpose we are using scikit-learn tool with TfidfVectorizer library which allows to make those activities into one step.
